About Me

I grew up in a small village called Ballya in India (place does not exit in wiki). My home is far from bustling city and also mobile coverage (and ofcourse internet)! The village is covered with lust ever-green forest on all sides and has amazing sun-rise views as it very close to Western Ghats. As you might have expected, we are basically agriculturist and grow variety of food products. The natives of the village speak a regional language called Tulu which dates back to several centuries. However, my mother tongue is Havyaka, a variant of old-kannada dialect. Oh, I forgot to mention, we live in harmony with elephants, leopards, snakes and peacocks (Indian Peafowl). So if you are wild life photographer, you found the right destination.
