About Me

I grew up in a small village called Ballya in India (place does not exit in wiki) . My home is far from bustling city and also mobile coverage (and internet)! The village is covered with lust ever-green forest on all sides and has amazing sun-rise views as it very close to Western Ghats. As you might have expected, we are basically agriculturist and grow variety of food products. The natives of the village speak a regional language called Tulu which dates back to several centuries. However, my mother tongue is Havyaka, a variant of old-kannada dialect.
